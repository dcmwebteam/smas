ABOUT

SMAS

Stevens Social Media maintains the strategic direction and development of all university-wide social accounts, as well as college, school and program presences. Stevens Social Media also maintains the official accounts inventory, provides consultation services and training and offers best practices. The office is focused on encouraging collaboration and promoting a unified campus message. Stevens Social Media staff members also oversee the daily content management of central social platforms and disseminate messaging that promotes university-related initiatives.
